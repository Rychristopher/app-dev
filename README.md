# 🌌 The Marvel Infinity Stone Saga

> *"I am... inevitable."* – Thanos  
> An epic journey across time, space, and emotions — the **Infinity Saga** is the crown jewel of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), spanning 23 films from *Iron Man* to *Avengers: Endgame*.

---

## 🪐 The Six Infinity Stones

| Stone         | Color    | Power                    | First Appearance                  |
|---------------|----------|--------------------------|------------------------------------|
| Space Stone   | 🔵 Blue  | Teleportation, space     | *Captain America: The First Avenger* |
| Mind Stone    | 🟡 Yellow| Intelligence, control    | *The Avengers*                     |
| Reality Stone | 🔴 Red   | Alter reality            | *Thor: The Dark World*             |
| Power Stone   | 🟣 Purple| Destruction, strength    | *Guardians of the Galaxy*          |
| Time Stone    | 🟢 Green | Time manipulation        | *Doctor Strange*                   |
| Soul Stone    | 🟠 Orange| Control over life & death| *Avengers: Infinity War*           |
